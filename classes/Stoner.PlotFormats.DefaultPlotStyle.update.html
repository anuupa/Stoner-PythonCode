DefaultPlotStyle.update¶

DefaultPlotStyle.update(**kargs)[source]¶

Update the template with new attributes from keyword arguments. Keyword arguments may be supplied to set default parameters. Any Matplotlib rc parameter may be specified, with .’s replaced with _ and )_ replaced with __.
