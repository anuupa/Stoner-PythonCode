Stoner.FileFormats.LSTemperatureFile¶

class Stoner.FileFormats.LSTemperatureFile(*args, **kargs)[source]¶

A class that reads and writes Lakeshore Temperature Calibration Curves.

Warning

THis class works for cernox curves in Log Ohms/Kelvin and Log Ohms/Log Kelvin. It may or may not work with any other temperature calibration data !
