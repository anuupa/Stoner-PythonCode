fmr_power¶

Stoner.Fit.fmr_power(H, H_res, Delta_H, K_1, K_2)[source]¶

A combination of a Lorentzian and differential Lorenztion peak as measured in an FMR experiment.

Args:
H (array) magnetic field data H_res (float): Resonance field of peak Delta_H (float): Preak wideth K_1, K_2 (float): Relative weighting of each component.
Returns:
Array of model absorption values.

math:`

rac{4 Delta_{H} K_{1} left(H - H_{res} ight)}{left(Delta_{H}^{2} + 4 left(H - H_{res} ight)^{2} ight)^{2}} - rac{K_{2} left(Delta_{H}^{2} - 4 left(H - H_{res} ight)^{2} ight)}{left(Delta_{H}^{2} + 4 left(H - H_{res} ight)^{2} ight)^{2}}`
