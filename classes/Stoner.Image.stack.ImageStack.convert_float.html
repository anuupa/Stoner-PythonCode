ImageStack.convert_float¶

ImageStack.convert_float(clip_negative=True)[source]¶

Convert the imarray to floating point type normalised to -1 to 1.

If clip_negative then clip intensities below 0 to 0.

Keyword Arguments:
 clip_negative (bool) – whether to clip intensities
