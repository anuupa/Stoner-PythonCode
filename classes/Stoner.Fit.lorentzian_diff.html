lorentzian_diff¶

Stoner.Fit.lorentzian_diff(x, A, sigma, mu)[source]¶

Implement a differential form of a Lorentzian peak.

Parameters:
  • x (array) – x data
  • A (flaot) – Peak amplitude
  • sigma (float) – peak wideth
  • mu (float) – peak location in x
  • Returns – \(\frac{A \sigma \left(2 \mu - 2 x\right)}{\pi \left(\sigma^{2} + \left(- \mu + x\right)^{2}\right)^{2}}\)
