Stoner.Fit.bdr(V, A, phi, dphi, d, mass)[source]¶

BDR model tunnelling V=bias voltage, params=[] A: in m^2, phi: average barrier height in eV, dphi: change in barrier height in eV, d: barrier width in angstrom, mass: effective electron mass as a fraction of electron rest mass

See Brinkman et. al. J. Appl. Phys. 41 1915 (1970) or Tuan Comm. in Phys. 16, 1, (2006)
